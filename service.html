Services

See what we do best

Fast Delivery

Thanks to the store network, Flumen estimates that the delivery will take less than 5 hops and, according to estimations a parcel can be delivered in less than a couple of hours.

Node Pick up

Users will be able to pickup or drop off the parcel at an affiliated node.

Scheduled delivery

In case users want to receive the parcel at home they can specify a time range in which they would like to receive the package at home.

Live Tracking

Users will be able to see on a map the location of their parcel. The position will be retrieved from the system that will return the node position when it is temporarily stored in a node or the messenger position when the parcel is being transported.

Easy Return Management

Users will be able to call on demand a messenger to their doorstep in order to return a parcel after the return has been acknowledged from the merchant. Return can be also exploited through the network of nodes.

Fast Support

Need something? Ask our chatbot or our customer service staff
